    General
    Ict Name
    The name of this Ict. It does not have any use currently.
    Round Duration (ms)
    A round is the heart beat of your Ict. It specifies the relative time intervals of other events such as the spam protection. Every round your neighbor stats are logged. (recommended: 60000)
    Tangle Capacity
    Your tangle will be stopped from growing too large by pruning old transactions. The tangle capacity specifies how many transactions can be stored in your tangle before old ones are removed. (recommended: 10000)
    Address
    Host
    The host allows your neighbors to connect to you. It can be your IP or a domain. (recommended: 0.0.0.0)
    Port
    The port on which Ict will exchange messages with neighbors. (default: 1337)
    Web GUI
    GUI Enabled
    Disabling this will stop this web GUI.
    GUI Port
    The port of this web GUI. If you change it, the page will disappear on this port. (default: 2187)
    GUI Password
    The port for this GUI. Make sure it is secure. Otherwise you Ict will be controlled by evil deamons. (default: 2187)
    Gossip
    Min. Forward Delay (ms)
    The minimum amount of milliseconds to wait before forwarding transactions to your neighbors.
    Max. Forward Delay (ms)
    The maximum amount of milliseconds to wait before forwarding transactions to your neighbors.
    Max. Abs. Tx./Round
    Spam protection: your Ict will not accept more than this many transactions per round from each neighbor.
